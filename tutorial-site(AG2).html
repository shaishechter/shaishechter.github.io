Algebraic Geometry 2 5780

Tutorial slides and notes


Session 1 : Recollections about noetherity, Hilbert's Basis Theorem, Nullstellensatz

Session 2 : Introduction to sheaves, stalks, morphisms

Session 3 : The image presheaf, Leray and Grothendieck sheaves, sheafification, operations on sheaves

Session 4 : Adjointness of pullback and direct image, spectrum of a ring, (locally) ringed spaces

Session 5 : Examples of morphisms of schemes, the functor of points approace, Yoneda lema

Session 6 : Functor of points, products and fibered products, models of schemes

Session 7 : Examples of non-affine schemes, the gluing lemma, first construction of projective n-space

Session 8 : Properties of schemes- connectedness, irreducibility, reducedness, integrality, noetherity, immersions and dimension

Session 9 : Morphisms (locally) of finite type, finite morphisms, the fiber of a morphism, more on dimension
