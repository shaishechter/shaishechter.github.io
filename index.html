Shai Shechter

About Me

I am a Post Doc in the faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at Weizmann Institute of Science Rehovot, Israel. My CV is available here.

I'm interested mainly in representation theory, mostly of arithmetic groups and their local completions and in subjects relating to representation growth.

I am part of Rami Aizenbud's research group.

I completed my PhD under the supervision of Uri Onn.

Teaching

Currently, I am a teaching assistant in the course Algebraic Geometry 2 (taught by Rami Aizenbud) at WIS. Tutorial notes and exercises are available here.

In previous years I acted as a teaching assistant of Generalized Functions, Disrete Mathematics, Algebra for CS and math and various Calculus courses for engineering, at BGU, as well as a lecturer of pre-Calculus for high school teachers.
